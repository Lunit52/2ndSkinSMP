How it's done.

An SMP treatment, step by step.

The Consultation.

This can be done in person at 2nd Skin Studio, but can often be handled via phone and email/text communication. You will be provided with a procedure plan and rate estimate.

Session One.

The purpose of this session is to create shape, style and definition in full scalp procedures, and to add the first layer of follicular density in full scalp, thinning hair and scar camouflage procedures. This procedure also helps us determine the proper shade of pigment, and assess how your skin reacts to the but it's not finished yet!

Session Two.

This is the WOW Session! Another layer of follicular densification is added, creating tighter, thicker follicles, and a more finished look. For some, this is their last session, however many require a third or even fourth session to achieve their desired result. This is more common for coverage of larger areas, or for clients with very dense and/or dark natural follicles.

Maintenance

This is the EASY part! Just don't sweat, shave or wash shalp for one week. That's it! After that, protect it with sunblock and we'll see you back for a touchup in 5-10 years!
